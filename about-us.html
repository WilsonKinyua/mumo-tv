Who We Are

Mumo Tv is a vibrant and dynamic 24-hour digital television broadcasting in Kikamba.

The free-to-air channel is dedicated to serve the Kamba community and any other person who understands the local language.

And therefore committed to provide the highest level of news updates coupled with a flash of other interactive shows.

The TV’s on-air medium

It can be accessed through:

  • Star times (PANG channel 20)
  • Signet channel
  • And soon we are coming on Zuku and Go TV platforms.

Mission Statement

Mumo Tv provides quality news updates, talk shows, inspirational programs and broadcasting dedicated to uplift the lives of the Kamba community so as to position them to get a share of the national cake.

The digital channel is best known and trusted source of news and empowerment programmes across ukambani and neighboring counties

Vision

To become a truly Kamba television channel that will provide the community with a platform to share ideas in politics, economy and social issues geared towards a positive life transformation.

Mumo TV programming

The channel focuses more on news updates while leaving a little share to entertainment.

Besides news there is agriculture show dubbed uthui wa muthanga,health show dubbed uima waku,women and youth empowerment shows, shows promoting the disabled, cultural shows, guidance and counseling shows and summons.

Features and Documentaries.

Entertainment shows features.

Target Audience

Mumo Tv targets Kamba persons of all ages- meaning there is a program for every age group.
Currently, the television’s reach is approximately three million people spread across Lower Eastern region (Machakos, Makueni, Kitui), Kajiado, Embu, Meru, Garissa, Nairobi and Mombasa.
