Post-Conference Sessions 8:30AM - 5:00PM
Fraud Strategy & Execution in the Digital Marketplace
In this workshop, we’ll take a deep dive in the world of MDES, the Mastercard Digital Enablement System, to understand the new data points issuers now have access to when digitizing cards on mobile devices. We’ll also discuss how to prevent, detect and mitigate fraud in this new payment space, as well as lessons learned from prior implementations. 		8:30AM - 1:00PM
The Future of Chargeback Processing
Join Mastercard and industry leaders to learn about chargeback trends and best business practices for optimizing performance in dispute resolution. This year we will particularly focus on strategies to reduce fraud-related chargebacks and the rise of counterfeit goods disputes. We will also host an in-depth discussion on the Dispute Resolution Initiative, Mastercard’s strategy for reducing chargeback volumes, shortening resolution time frames, and improving the experience for all affected parties.
  • Ever changing world of chargebacks – hear about the latest rule changes
  • New products and technologies – how do they impact liabilities between issuers and acquirers?
  • Fraud Trends and their impact on chargeback processing
  • Counterfeit goods disputes – Mastercard, customer and anti-piracy expert perspectives
  • Dispute Resolution Initiative – roadmap for improving the chargeback process
 		9:00AM - 5:00PM
